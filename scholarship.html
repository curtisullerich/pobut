Popcorn Button is all about growing the presence of music in the community. Using extra donations beyond our operating costs from our dances, we have started a small scholarship for pre-college students to help fund the cost of private lessons, band camp registration, or honor band registration. There are two deadlines per year for the Popcorn Button Community Music Scholarship: November 1st and May 1st. You can download the scholarship application here. Feel free to email info@popcornbutton.org with any questions. Want a poster to put up in your band room? Here you go!
